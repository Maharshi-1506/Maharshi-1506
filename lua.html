About Us

Meet Our Founder

Pritesh Mistry is a distinguished martial artist and the founder of Level Up Karate Academy, an esteemed institution under the banner of ISKU (International Shotokan Karate United). With over a decade of experience in the field of karate, Pritesh has dedicated his life to the promotion and teaching of this martial art. His expertise and passion have been instrumental in spreading the values and discipline of karate across various regions of Ahmedabad.

Pritesh believes in the holistic development of individuals through karate, emphasizing physical, mental, and spiritual growth. His personalized approach to training ensures each student receives tailored attention, fostering an environment of mutual respect and continuous improvement.

Community Work

Pritesh Mistry has shown a strong commitment to social responsibility by collaborating with various non-governmental organizations (NGOs). Through these collaborations, he has offered karate training as a tool for empowerment and self-defense, particularly targeting underprivileged sections of society. His community-oriented initiatives have provided numerous individuals with essential life skills and a sense of confidence.

Our community programs aim to make a positive impact and help individuals gain self-esteem and resilience through karate training.
