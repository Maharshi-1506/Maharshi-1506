PIZZA INFO

Pizza Specifications

  • Type: Margherita
  • Size: Medium
  • Crust: Thin Crust
  • Ingredients: Tomato Sauce, Mozzarella, Basil

Recipe

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough

Tomato Sauce

Mozzarella Cheese

Fresh Basil Leaves

Olive Oil

Salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475F (245C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
